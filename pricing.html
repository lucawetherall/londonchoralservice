Choir and singer pricing

Clear, transparent pricing. What you see is what you pay — no hidden fees, no surprises. Prices include all fees and taxes. Travel costs may apply for venues beyond Greater London.

What happens next

Tell us about your occasion and we’ll send you a clear, written quote tailored to your venue and repertoire. If you’d like to go ahead, we take care of everything from that point: rehearsals, sheet music, and full coordination with your venue or funeral director. You won’t need to chase us or manage logistics.

Your final quote may differ slightly depending on repertoire, venue requirements, or travel distance — we always confirm the price before you commit.
