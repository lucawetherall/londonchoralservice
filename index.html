What we offer

From a single soloist to a twelve-voice chorus, accompanied by pianists, organists, harpists, or string quartets — we provide live music tailored to the character of your occasion.

Whether you need a hymn sung beautifully at a funeral, a choir to fill a church at a wedding, or a soloist for an intimate memorial, we take care of every musical detail so you don't have to.

Who we are

Our roster of over 150 auditioned singers and instrumentalists includes graduates of the Royal Academy of Music and the Royal College of Music. They have performed at the Royal Albert Hall, St Paul's Cathedral, Westminster Abbey, the Royal Opera House, and the O2 Arena, among many other prestigious venues.

Their credits include the Rugby World Cup, the Olympics closing ceremony, the Queen's Golden Jubilee, and the coronation of King Charles III.
