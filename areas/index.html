Areas we serve across the United Kingdom

Based in London, available nationwide. Our conservatoire-trained singers, choirs, and instrumentalists perform at funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies in every corner of England — from cathedral cities to village churches, crematoriums to private estates.

London & the South East

  • London

    Our home base. Funeral singers, wedding choirs, and ceremony musicians across all London boroughs — from Westminster Abbey to local parish churches and crematoriums.

    Funeral and wedding music across Guildford and Surrey, including Guildford Cathedral and churches throughout the county.

  • Brighton

    Singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, and ceremonies in Brighton & Hove and across East Sussex.

  • Canterbury

    Ceremony musicians for Canterbury and Kent, from Canterbury Cathedral to parish churches and crematoriums across the county.

    Funeral singers and wedding choirs for Rochester, Medway, and surrounding areas of north Kent.

  • Chelmsford

    Professional musicians for funerals and weddings in Chelmsford and across Essex.

Thames Valley & the Home Counties

  • Reading

  • Slough & Maidenhead

  • Windsor

    Ceremony musicians for Windsor, Eton, and the surrounding Royal Borough.
