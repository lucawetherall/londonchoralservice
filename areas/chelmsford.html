Funeral singers and choirs in Chelmsford

Chelmsford, the county town of Essex, sits at the heart of a county rich in history, community, and quiet English beauty. Its excellent transport links from London mean that families and friends can gather easily from across the south-east, and its churches and chapels offer settings of real dignity and character. The London Choral Service provides professional singers, choirs, and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across Chelmsford and the whole of Essex, bringing the level of care and musicianship that these important occasions deserve.

Funeral music in Chelmsford

Losing someone you love changes everything, and we know that arranging a funeral while you are still in the early days of grief can feel almost impossible. We want you to know that we are here to lift the burden of the music from your shoulders entirely. Our singers perform at funerals and memorial services throughout Chelmsford and Essex — at Chelmsford Cathedral, the seat of the Bishop of Chelmsford and a church whose origins reach back to the thirteenth century, at St Mary’s Church, at Chelmsford Crematorium, at South Essex Crematorium, and at parish churches across the county. Whether you would like a single voice singing The Lord Is My Shepherd in a small chapel of rest, or a full choir bringing a congregation together with a great hymn, we will shape every detail around what feels right for you and for the person you are saying goodbye to.

Families in Essex often tell us that they want the music to feel personal — not grand for the sake of it, but genuinely fitting for the person being remembered. We understand that completely, and it is the conversation we have with every family we work with. Every musician on our roster is conservatoire-trained, and many have sung in cathedral and collegiate choirs. If you are unsure which music to choose, our funeral music guide offers thoughtful suggestions for hymns, solos, and choral works that families often find a real source of comfort. You are also very welcome to telephone us — we are always happy to talk things through at your own pace, and there is never any pressure or obligation.

Wedding choirs and singers

Chelmsford and the Essex countryside offer a wonderful variety of settings for a wedding — from the elegant interior of Chelmsford Cathedral, with its fine stained glass and warm acoustic, to the many beautiful medieval parish churches scattered through the villages and market towns that surround the city. Live singing brings any of these spaces to life in a way that no recording can. A choir filling a nave with harmony, a soprano performing during the signing of the register, a congregation singing a hymn they all know by heart — these are the moments that people carry with them long after the day itself. Our choirs and soloists perform at weddings of every size and denomination across Chelmsford and Essex, and we are always happy to learn new repertoire if there is a piece that holds particular meaning for you. Our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a wonderful place to start if you are still thinking about your options.

Ensembles & pricing

We offer everything from a single soloist to a full choir of twelve voices, and can pair singers with an organist, harpist, or string quartet. Each musician is personally selected from our handpicked roster of professional singers and instrumentalists. Because every occasion is different, we encourage you to tell us a little about what you have in mind so we can recommend the right ensemble and provide an accurate quote. Full details of our standard packages are available on our pricing page.
