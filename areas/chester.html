Funeral singers and choirs in Chester

The London Choral Service provides professional singers, choirs, and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across Chester and the wider Cheshire region. Chester’s medieval heritage — its ancient city walls, timber-framed Rows, and centuries of cathedral worship — has given the city a deep connection to sacred music, and there are few places in England where the sound of live voices feels more at home.

Funeral music in Chester

When you are grieving, the practicalities of arranging a funeral can feel relentless, and choosing the right music is often one of the hardest decisions. We are here to take that burden from you. Our singers perform at funerals and memorial services throughout Chester and Cheshire — at Chester Cathedral, whose magnificent red sandstone nave has provided solace to mourners for nearly a thousand years, at the ancient St John the Baptist Church with its atmospheric Norman arches and quiet dignity, and at Blacon Crematorium, where a single voice or small ensemble can bring extraordinary warmth to a more intimate farewell. We also regularly sing at parish churches across Cheshire, from village chapels in the Dee Valley to larger town churches in Northwich, Nantwich, and Macclesfield.

Every musician we work with is conservatoire-trained and accustomed to performing in settings where emotion is raw and the music truly matters. Whether you would like an alto singing Be Still, My Soul as the coffin is carried in, or a four-part choir to lead the congregation through Abide with Me, we will work with you and your funeral director to ensure every detail is right. If you are unsure where to begin, our funeral music guide suggests hymns, solos, and choral pieces that families have found genuinely comforting. You are also welcome to telephone us for a quiet, unhurried conversation — there is never any pressure, and we are glad to help at whatever pace you need.

Wedding choirs and singers

Chester is a glorious city in which to marry. The cathedral’s medieval quire stalls and sweeping nave offer a setting of real grandeur, while the city’s smaller churches provide warmth and character on a more personal scale. Beyond the walls, the Cheshire countryside is dotted with beautiful village churches, manor houses, and country estates that make wonderful wedding venues. Live singing elevates every one of these spaces. A choir filling the nave with a well-loved hymn, a tenor performing as the register is signed, guests joining together for a piece that everyone in the room already knows — these are the moments that couples tell us they remember most vividly, long after the photographs have been filed away.

We work with couples of every denomination and none, and we are always happy to learn new repertoire if a particular song or piece holds personal meaning for you. If you are still thinking about what music to have, our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a helpful place to start.

Ensembles & pricing

We offer a range of ensemble sizes to suit every occasion, from a solo singer or instrumentalist to a full choir of up to twelve voices. Organists, harpists, and string quartets are available for both sacred and secular settings across Chester and Cheshire. Each musician is hand-picked from our handpicked roster of professional singers and instrumentalists. Our pricing is clear and straightforward, with no hidden fees. Visit our pricing page for full details, or get in touch and we’ll put together a quote tailored to your event.
