Funeral singers and choirs in Guildford

The London Choral Service provides professional singers, choirs, and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across Guildford and the wider Surrey area. Every musician we work with is conservatoire-trained, and every performance is shaped with genuine care around your occasion and the people who matter most.

Funeral music in Guildford

When someone close to you dies, even the most practical tasks can feel impossible. Organising music for a funeral while you are still grieving is something no one should have to face alone, and we are here to take that weight from you entirely. Whether you’re planning a service beneath the soaring modernist arches of Guildford Cathedral, a more intimate farewell at Holy Trinity Church or St Nicolas’ Church in the centre of town, or a cremation at Guildford Crematorium, we will work gently with you and your funeral director to make sure the music reflects the person you have lost and brings comfort to everyone who gathers to remember them. A solo soprano singing Ave Maria as the congregation settles, a small choir lifting the words of a much-loved hymn into the stillness of a chapel, a cellist playing a quiet melody as family members take their seats — whatever feels right for your farewell, we will arrange it with sensitivity and warmth.

Our singers are experienced at performing in churches, chapels, and crematoria right across Surrey — from Woking and Farnham to Epsom, Dorking, Reigate, Cobham, and Haslemere. Many of the families who contact us are unsure where to begin with the music, and that is completely natural. There is no wrong question, and there is never any pressure. We are always happy to talk things through, suggest pieces that suit the setting, and help you find music that will feel meaningful on the day. You can explore our full range of funeral music services, or browse our guide to funeral music for ideas on hymns, solos, and instrumental pieces that work beautifully in both church and crematorium settings.

Wedding music in Guildford

Surrey is home to some of the loveliest wedding venues in the south of England, and live music can transform any ceremony into something truly unforgettable. From the grandeur of Guildford Cathedral and the warm, candlelit intimacy of Holy Trinity Church to the honey-coloured village churches tucked into the Surrey Hills, there is a richness to hearing real voices fill these spaces that no recording can replicate. Our choirs and soloists perform at every stage of the day — the processional as you walk down the aisle, the hymns that draw every voice in the room together, a reflective piece during the signing of the register, and a joyful recessional to carry you out into your new life together. We work closely with every couple to choose music that feels genuinely personal, whether that means traditional favourites, a contemporary arrangement, or a piece with deep sentimental meaning that only you and your partner will fully understand.

If you’re still deciding on your wedding music, our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a helpful starting point. It covers the most popular choices alongside a few less expected suggestions that our couples have loved.

Ensembles & pricing

We offer a range of ensemble sizes to suit every venue and occasion, from a solo singer or instrumentalist to a full choir of up to twelve voices. String quartets, harpists, and organists are also available for both sacred and secular settings across Guildford and Surrey. Our pricing is clear and straightforward, with no hidden extras. Visit our pricing page for full details, or get in touch and we’ll put together a quote tailored to exactly what you need.
