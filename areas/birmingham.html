Funeral singers and choirs in Birmingham

The London Choral Service provides professional singers, choirs, and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across Birmingham and the wider West Midlands. Every musician we work with is conservatoire-trained, and every performance is shaped around the occasion and the people it’s for.

Funeral music in Birmingham

Arranging music for a funeral is one of those tasks that can feel overwhelming at an already difficult time. We’re here to make it simple. Whether you’re planning a service at Birmingham Cathedral (St Philip’s), a requiem mass at St Martin in the Bull Ring, or a cremation service at Lodge Hill Crematorium, Perry Barr Crematorium, or Sutton Coldfield Crematorium, we’ll work with you and your funeral director to ensure the music is exactly right. A single soprano singing Pie Jesu, a small choir performing a favourite hymn, a cellist playing as people gather — whatever feels fitting, we can arrange it.

Many families come to us unsure of what to choose, and that’s completely normal. We’re happy to talk through the options and suggest pieces that suit the tone of the service. You can explore our full range of funeral music services, or browse our guide to funeral music for ideas on hymns, solos, and instrumental pieces that work beautifully in both church and crematorium settings.

Wedding music in Birmingham

Birmingham has no shortage of wonderful wedding venues, from the grandeur of Birmingham Cathedral and the ornate interiors of the Birmingham Oratory to intimate civil ceremonies at the city’s historic hotels and country houses in the surrounding Warwickshire countryside. Our choirs and soloists bring warmth and presence to every part of the day — from the processional as you walk down the aisle to the hymns, the signing of the register, and the recessional. We work closely with couples to choose music that reflects who they are, whether that means traditional choral hymns, a contemporary arrangement, or something unexpected.

If you’re still deciding on your music, our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a good place to start. It covers the most popular choices along with a few less obvious suggestions that our couples have loved.

Ensembles & pricing

We offer a range of ensemble sizes to suit every venue and budget, from a solo singer or instrumentalist to a full choir of up to twelve voices. String quartets, harpists, and organists are also available for both sacred and secular settings across Birmingham. Our pricing is straightforward and transparent, with no hidden fees. Visit our pricing page for full details, or get in touch and we’ll put together a quote tailored to your occasion.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Birmingham?

A solo funeral singer in Birmingham starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within the local area and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Birmingham?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Birmingham — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the area. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Birmingham?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Birmingham?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums in and around Birmingham. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
