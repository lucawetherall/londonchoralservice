Funeral singers and choirs in London

London is our home. With over 150 conservatoire-trained singers, choirs, and instrumentalists based across the capital, we provide live music for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies in every London borough — from historic cathedrals to local crematoriums, hotel ballrooms to quiet parish churches.

Funeral music across London

When a family loses someone, the music at the funeral can hold the room together in a way that nothing else quite can. We work with families across London to provide singers and musicians who bring warmth, dignity, and beauty to the service — whether that’s a solo voice singing Pie Jesu at a crematorium, a quartet of voices leading hymns at a parish church, or a full choir for a cathedral requiem.

Our musicians regularly perform at venues throughout London, including Westminster Abbey, St Paul’s Cathedral, Southwark Cathedral, Brompton Oratory, and churches in every borough. We also provide music at crematoriums across the capital, including City of London Crematorium, Mortlake Crematorium, and Putney Vale Crematorium. Every venue has its own acoustic and its own atmosphere, and our musicians know how to work sensitively within each one. If you’re not sure where to begin with choosing music, our guide to funeral music may help, or you’re welcome to get in touch and we’ll talk it through with you. You can also read more about our full range of funeral music services.

Wedding choirs in London

London offers an extraordinary range of wedding venues, from grand churches and historic chapels to licensed hotels and private gardens. Whatever the setting, live choral music can transform the ceremony into something truly unforgettable. Our wedding choirs and soloists lead congregational hymns with confidence, perform during the signing of the register, and set the tone for the whole day — whether you want the full Anglican choral tradition or something more intimate and personal.

We’ve sung at weddings in venues across London, from St Martin-in-the-Fields and the Temple Church to Chelsea Old Church and smaller parish churches in every corner of the city. If you’re still deciding on hymns or music, our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a good place to start.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
