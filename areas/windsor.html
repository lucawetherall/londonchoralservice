Funeral singers and choirs in Windsor

Windsor has stood at the heart of royal and ceremonial life in England for close to a thousand years. Its castle, its chapels, and its churches have been the setting for some of the most significant occasions in the nation’s history, and the tradition of fine music in these spaces stretches back just as far. The London Choral Service provides professional singers, choirs, and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across Windsor and the Royal Borough, bringing a level of care and musicianship that matches the dignity of these extraordinary surroundings.

Funeral music in Windsor

In the days after losing someone you love, everything feels fragile, and the thought of organising music for a funeral can be one worry too many. We are here so that you do not have to carry that alone. Our singers perform at funerals and memorial services throughout Windsor and the surrounding area — at St George’s Chapel, the breathtaking Gothic chapel within the walls of Windsor Castle where monarchs have been laid to rest for centuries; at Windsor Parish Church of St John the Baptist, a warm and welcoming church at the foot of the castle; at Holy Trinity Church in the old town; at Slough Crematorium nearby; and at parish churches across the Royal Borough. Whether you would like a lone tenor singing Ave Maria as mourners take their seats, a small choir performing Thou Knowest, Lord during the service, or the whole congregation joining together for Abide with Me, we listen carefully to what matters to you and build everything around it.

Windsor’s long royal history gives its churches a particular grandeur, and families often feel that the music should rise to meet it. We understand that instinct completely. Every musician on our roster is conservatoire-trained, and many have performed in the chapel choirs and concert halls that define English choral life. If you are uncertain about what music to choose, our funeral music guide offers gentle, practical suggestions for hymns, solos, and choral works that families have found a genuine source of solace. Please do feel free to telephone us whenever it suits you — we are always happy to talk, and there is never any obligation.

Wedding choirs and singers

There are very few places in the world where the setting for a wedding carries as much romance and history as Windsor. The fan-vaulted magnificence of St George’s Chapel, the handsome stone interior of Windsor Parish Church, the quiet charm of Holy Trinity, and the many beautiful churches that dot the Berkshire countryside beyond the town all provide backdrops that couples and their guests never forget. Live singing is what makes these spaces truly come alive. A choir’s harmony soaring up through a vaulted ceiling, a soprano’s voice ringing out during the signing of the register, an entire congregation singing a beloved hymn together with real conviction — these are the moments that stay with people for the rest of their lives. Our choirs and soloists perform at weddings of every size, faith, and tradition across Windsor, Eton, and the wider Royal Borough, and we are always glad to learn new music if there is a piece that holds special meaning for you. Our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a lovely place to begin if you are still thinking about your music.

Ensembles & pricing

We offer everything from a single soloist to a full choir of twelve voices, and can pair singers with an organist, harpist, or string quartet. Each musician is personally selected from our handpicked roster of professional singers and instrumentalists. Because every occasion is different, we encourage you to tell us a little about what you have in mind so we can recommend the right ensemble and provide an accurate quote. Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Windsor?

A solo funeral singer in Windsor starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within the local area and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Windsor?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Windsor — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the area. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Windsor?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Windsor?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums in and around Windsor. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
