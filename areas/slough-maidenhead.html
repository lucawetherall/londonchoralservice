Funeral singers and choirs in Slough and Maidenhead

Slough and Maidenhead sit along the Thames in the eastern reaches of Berkshire, close to Windsor and within easy reach of London. The London Choral Service provides professional singers, choirs, and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across both towns and the surrounding communities. Every musician we work with is conservatoire-trained, and every performance is thoughtfully shaped around the occasion and the people at its heart.

Funeral music in Slough and Maidenhead

In the days after someone dies, there is a long list of things that need to be done, and very little energy with which to do them. Choosing the right music for a funeral is one of those tasks that can feel especially daunting, because it matters so much and because you want to get it right. That is exactly where we come in. Our singers perform at funerals and memorial services throughout Slough, Maidenhead, and the wider area — at St Mary’s Slough, whose parish roots reach back to Norman times, at All Saints Maidenhead with its fine Victorian nave, at Slough Crematorium, and at Chilterns Crematorium near Amersham. We also cover churches and chapels in Burnham, Taplow, Cookham, Langley, and Iver, as well as the neighbouring parishes closer to Windsor.

A tenor singing Ave Maria as the congregation settles, a quartet of voices lifting Abide with Me so that even those who cannot sing through their tears still hear the words, a harpist playing gently during a moment of reflection — these are the things that stay with people long after the day itself. If you are unsure what music to choose, that is completely natural. Many of the families we work with feel the same way at first. Our funeral music guide offers gentle suggestions, and you are always welcome to call us for an unhurried conversation. There is never any pressure, and never any obligation.

Wedding music in Slough and Maidenhead

This stretch of the Thames Valley is full of charm, and couples here are fortunate to have a wide choice of wedding venues. The handsome churches of Maidenhead and the pretty villages along the river at Cookham and Bray offer quintessentially English settings, while the proximity to Windsor means the grandeur of the royal borough is just minutes away. For civil ceremonies at riverside hotels and country estates, live music brings a warmth and emotional presence that no playlist can replicate. Our choirs and soloists perform at every stage of the ceremony — a processional that fills the room with anticipation, hymns that draw every voice together, a tender solo during the signing of the register, and a recessional that sends you out into the day with joy.

We take the time to understand what matters to each couple, whether that means well-known traditional hymns, a contemporary arrangement of a song with personal significance, or a piece of choral music you first heard and never forgot. Our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a useful starting point if you are still deciding, with popular favourites alongside a few less familiar choices that our couples have loved.

Ensembles & pricing

We offer a range of ensemble sizes to suit every setting and budget, from a solo singer or instrumentalist to a full choir of up to twelve voices. String quartets, harpists, and organists are also available for both sacred and secular venues across Slough, Maidenhead, and the surrounding area. Our pricing is straightforward and transparent — no hidden fees, no surprises. Visit our pricing page for full details, or get in touch and we’ll put together a quote tailored to your occasion.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Slough & Maidenhead?

A solo funeral singer in Slough & Maidenhead starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within the local area and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Slough & Maidenhead?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Slough & Maidenhead — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the area. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Slough & Maidenhead?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Slough & Maidenhead?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums in and around Slough & Maidenhead. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
