Funeral singers and choirs in St Albans

The London Choral Service provides professional singers, choirs, and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across St Albans and the surrounding Hertfordshire countryside. With its magnificent cathedral and abbey church standing watch over the city for nearly a thousand years, St Albans has a long and unbroken tradition of sacred music — and there is something deeply fitting about live voices continuing that tradition at the moments that matter most.

Funeral music in St Albans

Choosing music for a funeral is one of those tasks that can feel impossibly heavy when you are already carrying so much. We are here to make it easier. Our singers perform at funerals and memorial services throughout St Albans and Hertfordshire — at St Albans Cathedral and Abbey Church, where the vast Norman nave and medieval wall paintings create a setting of quiet grandeur, at the beautiful St Stephen’s Church on the old Watling Street, at West Hertfordshire Crematorium, and at parish churches across the county from Harpenden and Hatfield to Berkhamsted and Hemel Hempstead. Whether you would like a solo soprano singing Pie Jesu as mourners gather, or a small choir to carry the congregation through The Lord is My Shepherd, we shape everything around what the family needs.

There is a tenderness to live singing that recorded music simply cannot offer, and in the resonant, ancient spaces that St Albans is blessed with, the effect can be profoundly moving. Every musician on our roster is conservatoire-trained and experienced in performing with sensitivity at occasions where the emotions in the room run deep. If you are not sure which music would be right, our funeral music guide offers gentle suggestions for hymns, solos, and choral works that other families have found a real source of comfort. You are also very welcome to call us simply to talk things through — there is never any obligation, and we will go at whatever pace feels right for you.

Wedding choirs and singers

St Albans is a beautiful place to be married. The cathedral’s immense nave — one of the longest in England — offers a sense of occasion that few venues can rival, while the city’s parish churches provide warmth and intimacy on a more personal scale. Beyond the city itself, Hertfordshire is full of charming village churches, country houses, and rural estates that make perfect settings for a wedding celebration. Live music brings all of these spaces to life. A choir raising the roof with a well-loved hymn, a mezzo-soprano performing during the signing of the register, guests joining in with a piece that means something to everyone present — these are the moments that couples and their families tell us they treasure most.

Our choirs and soloists perform at weddings of every size and denomination, and we are always glad to learn a new piece if a particular song holds special meaning for you. If you are still deciding on your music, our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a helpful place to begin.

Ensembles & pricing

We offer everything from a solo singer or instrumentalist to a full choir of up to twelve voices, and can pair singers with an organist, harpist, or string quartet. Each musician is personally selected from our roster of over 150 conservatoire-trained performers. Because every occasion is different, we encourage you to tell us a little about what you have in mind so we can suggest the right ensemble and provide an accurate quote. Full details of our standard packages are available on our pricing page.
