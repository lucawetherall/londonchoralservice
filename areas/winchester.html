Funeral singers and choirs in Winchester

Winchester is one of the most historically significant cities in England. Once the capital of the Anglo-Saxon kingdom of Wessex and seat of King Alfred the Great, its ancient streets, water meadows, and magnificent cathedral have drawn people here for well over a thousand years. The London Choral Service provides professional singers, choirs, and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across Winchester and Hampshire, bringing music of the highest quality to occasions that matter most.

Funeral music in Winchester

Arranging music for a funeral is one of those tasks that can feel impossibly difficult when you are grieving. We are here to make it as gentle and straightforward as possible, so you can focus entirely on your family. Our singers perform at funerals and memorial services throughout Winchester and the surrounding county — at Winchester Cathedral, one of the largest cathedrals in Europe and a building whose soaring nave lends an extraordinary sense of occasion to any service; at the beautiful and ancient St Cross Hospital & Church, tucked away beside the water meadows south of the city; at parish churches across Hampshire; at Southampton Crematorium; and at Basingstoke Crematorium. Whether you would like a solo soprano singing Pie Jesu as mourners gather, a small choir for a setting of The Lord Is My Shepherd, or a larger ensemble with strings for a formal memorial, we shape every detail around the person you are honouring and what feels right for your family.

Winchester’s ancient history gives its churches a depth of atmosphere that families often find deeply comforting. Many of these buildings have held funeral services for centuries, and the presence of beautiful live music can make a service feel truly worthy of the person you have lost. Every musician on our roster is conservatoire-trained, and many have sung in cathedral and collegiate choirs. If you are not sure where to begin with the music, our funeral music guide offers thoughtful suggestions for hymns, solos, and choral pieces that families find a genuine source of comfort. You are very welcome to telephone us at any time — we are always glad to talk things through, and there is never any obligation.

Wedding choirs and singers

There are few places in England more beautiful to be married than Winchester. The vast medieval nave of Winchester Cathedral, the intimate charm of St Cross Hospital & Church, and the many lovely parish churches scattered through the Hampshire countryside all offer settings that will stay with your guests for a lifetime. Live singing lifts these already remarkable spaces into something truly unforgettable. A choir filling the cathedral with harmony, a tenor performing during the signing of the register, the whole congregation raising their voices in a hymn everyone knows — these are the moments that people remember long after the day itself. Our choirs and soloists perform at weddings of every size and tradition across Winchester and the wider county, and we are always happy to learn new pieces if there is a song that holds particular meaning for you. Our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a wonderful place to start if you are still deciding on your music.

Ensembles & pricing

We offer everything from a single soloist to a full choir of twelve voices, and can pair singers with an organist, harpist, or string quartet. Each musician is personally selected from our handpicked roster of professional singers and instrumentalists. Because every occasion is different, we encourage you to tell us a little about what you have in mind so we can recommend the right ensemble and provide an accurate quote. Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Winchester?

A solo funeral singer in Winchester starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within the local area and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Winchester?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Winchester — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the area. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Winchester?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Winchester?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums in and around Winchester. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
