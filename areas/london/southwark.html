Funeral singers and choirs in Southwark

Southwark stretches from the Thames at London Bridge deep into the leafy streets of Dulwich and Peckham, encompassing some of London’s oldest and most atmospheric places of worship. We provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies at venues throughout the borough.

Funeral music in Southwark

The London Borough of Southwark is home to two cathedrals, each with a distinguished musical tradition. Southwark Cathedral, standing beside Borough Market on the south bank of the Thames, has been a place of worship for over a thousand years and its Gothic nave provides a resonant and deeply moving setting for funeral and memorial services. Across the borough, the Roman Catholic St George’s Cathedral on Lambeth Road offers an equally striking space for requiem masses and funeral liturgies. Our singers are experienced in both Anglican and Catholic traditions and can adapt their repertoire to suit any form of service. For guidance on selecting music, please see our funeral music guide or get in touch directly.

Beyond the cathedrals, we regularly provide funeral singers for services at St Giles Camberwell — a handsome Victorian church with a warm acoustic — and at Honor Oak Crematorium, which serves families from across South London. The quiet beauty of Dulwich Chapel, set among the grounds of Dulwich College, is another venue where our musicians have been privileged to perform. Whatever the setting, we work closely with funeral directors, clergy, and families to ensure that every detail of the music is handled with care and sensitivity. You can learn more about our approach on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Southwark

Southwark offers couples a wonderful variety of wedding venues, from the grandeur of Southwark Cathedral to the intimate charm of smaller parish churches in Dulwich, Camberwell, and Bermondsey. A live choir can transform a wedding ceremony, filling even the largest space with warmth and beauty. Our singers are skilled at supporting congregational hymn singing while also delivering polished choral performances during the signing of the register or as guests arrive. Whether you are planning a traditional church wedding or a more contemporary celebration, we will help you choose the perfect repertoire. Browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns for inspiration.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
