Funeral singers and choirs in Richmond upon Thames

The London Borough of Richmond upon Thames is widely regarded as one of the most beautiful parts of London, with its royal parks, Thames-side walks, and handsome period architecture. The London Choral Service provides conservatoire-trained singers and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across Richmond, Twickenham, Kew, Ham, and the surrounding areas.

Funeral music in Richmond upon Thames

We regularly perform at services throughout the borough, including St Mary Magdalene Richmond — a landmark church overlooking the Thames — Mortlake Crematorium, and St Anne’s Kew. Our singers also work at churches and chapels in Ham, Twickenham, Teddington, and Barnes, bringing the same care and professionalism to every service. Whether you would like a soloist singing a reflective piece or a quartet leading the congregation in traditional hymns, we help you find music that feels right for the person you are remembering. Our funeral music guide offers a wide range of suggestions, and we encourage you to get in touch to discuss your plans.

Mortlake Crematorium serves a wide area of south-west London, and our singers know its chapels well. We understand the particular timing constraints of crematorium services and work efficiently within them, ensuring that every note counts. Families often tell us that live singing brought a sense of occasion and tenderness that they had not expected. You can find out more about how we prepare for each service on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Richmond upon Thames

Richmond upon Thames is one of London’s most sought-after wedding locations, and it is easy to see why. The Tudor splendour of Hampton Court Chapel, the riverside elegance of St Mary Magdalene, and the botanical beauty of Kew all provide extraordinary settings for a marriage ceremony. Our singers can perform at any of these venues, as well as at the many licensed venues in Twickenham, Ham, and beyond. From triumphant hymns to tender motets, we work with each couple to create a musical programme that enhances the joy of the day. Our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a good place to start.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Richmond?

A solo funeral singer in Richmond starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Richmond?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Richmond — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Richmond?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Richmond?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Richmond. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
