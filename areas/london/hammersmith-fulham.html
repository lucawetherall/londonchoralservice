Funeral singers and choirs in Hammersmith & Fulham

The London Borough of Hammersmith and Fulham stretches along the north bank of the Thames, home to a rich mix of Victorian churches, riverside chapels, and historic estates. The London Choral Service provides conservatoire-trained singers and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies throughout the borough.

Funeral music in Hammersmith & Fulham

Whether you are planning a church funeral at St Paul’s Hammersmith, a service at Fulham Palace Chapel, or a ceremony at All Saints Fulham, our singers bring a depth of feeling that recorded music simply cannot match. We also regularly perform at Margravine Cemetery and at the borough’s smaller neighbourhood churches, adapting our repertoire to reflect the wishes of the family. From traditional hymns and psalms to contemporary songs, we can help you choose music that feels right — our funeral music guide is a helpful starting point, and our team is always available to talk things through. Please get in touch whenever you are ready.

Our singers are experienced in working alongside funeral directors, clergy, and celebrants across Hammersmith and Fulham. We understand the importance of punctuality, discretion, and sensitivity on the day, and we take care of every musical detail so that you don’t have to. Whether the service is religious, secular, or a blend of both, we shape our programme around what matters to you. Learn more about what we offer on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Hammersmith & Fulham

Hammersmith and Fulham offers some truly distinctive settings for a wedding ceremony. The grand interior of St Paul’s Hammersmith suits a full choir and organ, while the intimate Fulham Palace Chapel — set within the beautiful grounds of the Bishop’s Palace — is perfect for a smaller ensemble. Our singers can also perform at licensed venues along the river and at local register offices. We help couples select hymns, anthems, and songs that set the tone for their day, and our guide to choosing wedding hymns is full of practical advice for those just starting to plan.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
