Funeral singers and choirs in Hounslow

The London Borough of Hounslow spans a broad sweep of west London, from the Georgian elegance of Chiswick to the green open spaces of Feltham and Isleworth. The London Choral Service provides conservatoire-trained singers and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies at venues throughout the borough.

Funeral music in Hounslow

Our singers perform regularly at churches and crematoria across Hounslow, including St Mary’s Hounslow, West London Crematorium in Hanwell, and parish churches in Feltham, Chiswick, and Isleworth. Each venue has its own character and acoustic, and we take care to match our ensemble and repertoire to the setting. Whether a family requests a single voice singing Ave Maria or a quartet leading a congregation through well-loved hymns, we bring the same commitment to musical excellence and pastoral care. Our funeral music guide offers suggestions across many traditions, and we are always glad to help you plan. Please contact us whenever you are ready.

We work closely with funeral directors and officiants across the borough to ensure that the musical elements of a service are coordinated with care. Our singers arrive early, dress appropriately, and conduct themselves with the utmost professionalism. For many families, live singing transforms a funeral from an ordeal into something genuinely comforting — a moment of shared beauty in the midst of grief. You can read more about how we approach each booking on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Hounslow

From the handsome churches of Chiswick to Isleworth’s charming riverside setting, Hounslow offers a surprisingly varied range of wedding venues. Our singers can provide rousing congregational hymns, a reflective anthem during the signing of the register, or uplifting music as the couple leave the church. We also perform at civil ceremonies in licensed venues across the borough, bringing the warmth and polish of live choral music to every celebration. Browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns for ideas on building your perfect programme.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
