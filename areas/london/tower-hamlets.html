Funeral singers and choirs in Tower Hamlets

The London Borough of Tower Hamlets lies at the historic heart of London’s East End, encompassing Stepney, Bow, Whitechapel, Spitalfields, Limehouse, and the Isle of Dogs. Our professional singers bring polished, heartfelt music to funerals, weddings, and memorial services at the borough’s remarkable churches and ceremony venues.

Funeral music in Tower Hamlets

Tower Hamlets is home to some of London’s most historically significant churches, many of which provide extraordinary settings for funeral services. St Dunstan’s Stepney, known as the Mother Church of the East End, has stood on its site for over a thousand years and possesses a warm, dignified acoustic that suits choral singing perfectly. Nicholas Hawksmoor’s Christ Church Spitalfields is one of London’s great Baroque churches — its vast, luminous interior can be profoundly moving during a funeral, and the acoustic carries voices with remarkable clarity. St Anne’s Limehouse, another Hawksmoor masterpiece overlooking the Thames, offers a similarly grand but intimate atmosphere. For families seeking something truly unique, the Chapel Royal of St Peter ad Vincula within the Tower of London is an extraordinary venue with centuries of history.

We provide music for funerals of every kind, whether you are planning a full choral service with traditional hymns or a quieter, more personal ceremony with a soloist singing a favourite piece. Our funeral music guide offers suggestions to help you get started, and our services page explains the options available. Please contact us to discuss your requirements — we are here to help at every stage.

Wedding choirs in Tower Hamlets

The great churches of Tower Hamlets are increasingly popular as wedding venues, and it is easy to understand why. Christ Church Spitalfields offers a truly spectacular backdrop for a wedding ceremony, and our choirs have performed there to wonderful effect. St Anne’s Limehouse, with its riverside setting and soaring columns, is another outstanding choice. Beyond the churches, Tower Hamlets has a growing number of licensed wedding venues in converted warehouses, galleries, and hotels where a vocal ensemble can provide the perfect accompaniment. Our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a helpful resource for couples beginning to plan their music.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Tower Hamlets?

A solo funeral singer in Tower Hamlets starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Tower Hamlets?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Tower Hamlets — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Tower Hamlets?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Tower Hamlets?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Tower Hamlets. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
