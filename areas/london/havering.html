Funeral singers and choirs in Havering

Havering sits on London’s eastern edge, where the city gives way to the green belt countryside of Essex. From the market town character of Romford and the village charm of Upminster to the quieter streets of Hornchurch and Corbets Tey, we provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across the borough.

Funeral music in Havering

South Essex Crematorium in Corbets Tey is the principal crematorium serving families in Havering and the surrounding area. Its chapels are well suited to live music, and our singers have performed there on many occasions, adapting their repertoire to reflect the full range of musical tastes — from traditional hymns and psalm settings to popular songs and modern classical works. St Andrew’s Church in Hornchurch, a handsome medieval parish church with a fine acoustic, is another venue we know well. Its spacious nave allows choral sound to develop naturally, creating a deeply moving atmosphere for funeral and memorial services. For help with choosing the right music, please see our funeral music guide or get in touch directly.

We also regularly provide funeral singers for services in Romford, Upminster, and the smaller churches and chapels that serve Havering’s communities. Whether the setting is a large parish church with a pipe organ or a modern crematorium chapel, we work closely with funeral directors, clergy, and families to ensure every detail is handled with care and sensitivity. Our singers are comfortable in services of all faiths and denominations, and we are happy to prepare unfamiliar repertoire when requested. You can learn more about our approach on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Havering

Havering’s churches offer couples some truly lovely settings for a wedding ceremony. St Andrew’s Hornchurch, with its ancient tower and peaceful churchyard, is a popular choice, while the churches of Upminster and Romford each have their own distinctive character. A live choir transforms the atmosphere of any wedding, adding a richness and warmth that stays with guests long after the day itself. Our singers are experienced at leading congregational hymns with energy and confidence while delivering refined choral performances during quieter moments. Browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns for inspiration.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Havering?

A solo funeral singer in Havering starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Havering?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Havering — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Havering?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Havering?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Havering. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
