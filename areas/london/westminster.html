Funeral singers and choirs in Westminster

The City of Westminster is home to some of Britain’s most celebrated churches and ceremonial spaces. We provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, and memorial services at venues throughout the borough.

Funeral music in Westminster

Westminster’s churches offer extraordinary settings for funeral and memorial services, and our singers are privileged to perform in many of them regularly. From the soaring nave of Westminster Abbey to the intimate beauty of St Margaret’s Westminster, each venue demands musicians who understand both the acoustic and the occasion. Our experienced ensembles bring sensitivity and skill to every service, whether it is a large public tribute or a quiet family farewell.

We regularly provide funeral singers for services at St James’s Piccadilly, the Guards’ Chapel at Wellington Barracks, and St Martin-in-the-Fields on Trafalgar Square. Many of our singers have trained or held positions in Westminster’s choral foundations, and they bring that depth of experience to every performance. We can also provide musicians for services at St Clement Danes on the Strand and the many other parish churches across the borough.

If you are planning a funeral or memorial service in Westminster and would like guidance on choosing music, please see our funeral music guide or get in touch to discuss your requirements.

Wedding choirs in Westminster

A Westminster wedding is a truly special occasion, and live choral music can elevate it into something unforgettable. Whether you are exchanging vows at St Margaret’s Westminster, celebrating at St James’s Piccadilly, or hosting an intimate ceremony at one of the borough’s smaller churches, our wedding choirs and vocal ensembles will bring warmth and grandeur to your day. We work closely with couples to select the perfect repertoire — from traditional hymns to contemporary arrangements. For inspiration, browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns.

Our singers are equally at home accompanying a congregation of hundreds or performing as a standalone ensemble for a more intimate gathering. We liaise directly with organists, vergers, and venue coordinators to ensure everything runs smoothly on the day.

Ensembles for every occasion

We offer flexible ensemble sizes to suit every venue and budget in Westminster — from a solo vocalist or duet for a simple ceremony to a full choir for a grand service at Westminster Abbey. All our musicians are professional singers drawn from London’s leading choral groups. For details on ensemble options and fees, please visit our pricing page.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Westminster?

A solo funeral singer in Westminster starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Westminster?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Westminster — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Westminster?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Westminster?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Westminster. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
