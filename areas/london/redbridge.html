Funeral singers and choirs in Redbridge

The London Borough of Redbridge spans the leafy suburbs of Wanstead and South Woodford through to the busy high streets of Ilford, Barkingside, and Seven Kings. We provide conservatoire-trained singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies at venues throughout the borough.

Funeral music in Redbridge

Forest Park Crematorium, situated on the border of Redbridge and Newham, is a modern facility with well-appointed chapels that our singers know intimately. Its clean acoustic responds well to both solo voices and small choral ensembles, and we take care to ensure that every performance is tailored to the family’s wishes. St Mary’s Church in Ilford, a substantial parish church with a warm and generous sound, is another venue where we regularly provide funeral music, as are churches in Barkingside and Seven Kings serving the borough’s diverse communities. For guidance on selecting appropriate music, please see our funeral music guide or get in touch directly.

The elegant village atmosphere of Wanstead, with its fine Georgian church of St Mary the Virgin overlooking the green, provides a particularly beautiful setting for funeral and memorial services. Whether the service is held in a historic church, a modern crematorium, or a community space, we work closely with funeral directors, clergy, and families to handle every musical detail with care. Our singers are experienced in services of all traditions and denominations, and we are always happy to learn specific pieces on request. You can learn more about our approach on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Redbridge

Redbridge offers couples a range of appealing wedding venues, from the handsome St Mary the Virgin in Wanstead — one of the most sought-after wedding churches in East London — to the intimate churches of Barkingside and the surrounding area. A live choir brings an unmistakable sense of occasion to any ceremony, filling the space with warmth and lifting the spirits of everyone present. Our singers are equally confident leading congregational hymns and performing intricate choral works during quieter moments of the service. Browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns for inspiration.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
