Funeral singers and choirs in Lewisham

The London Borough of Lewisham encompasses the diverse communities of Lewisham, Catford, Blackheath, and Grove Park. We provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies at churches, crematoria, and cemeteries throughout the borough.

Funeral music in Lewisham

Lewisham’s crematoria and churches are places where families gather to say goodbye, and our role is to ensure the music honours the occasion with dignity and beauty. Hither Green Crematorium is one of South London’s busiest and most well-known crematoria, and we have provided singers there on many occasions, understanding the precise timings and gentle approach that crematorium services require. Lewisham Crematorium, located in the south of the borough, offers a similarly peaceful setting for farewell services. At both venues, even a single trained voice can bring a remarkable depth of feeling to a short ceremony. For advice on choosing suitable music, please see our funeral music guide or get in touch to talk through your wishes.

For church funerals, St Mary’s Lewisham provides a handsome and welcoming space with a long history of serving the local community. Grove Park Cemetery is another venue where families often request live music for graveside services or committal rites, and our singers are experienced in performing outdoors with poise and sensitivity. We liaise directly with funeral directors and clergy to ensure that every practical detail is taken care of, leaving you free to focus on what matters most. You can read more about our approach on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Lewisham

Whether you are planning a grand celebration at one of Lewisham’s larger churches or an intimate ceremony in a smaller parish setting, live choral music will bring a special quality to your wedding day. The churches of Blackheath and Lee offer particularly attractive venues, with fine acoustics and beautiful interiors. Our singers will work with you to create a programme of hymns, anthems, and choral pieces that reflects your personality and the spirit of the day. For ideas on building the perfect musical programme, explore our guide to choosing wedding hymns.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Lewisham?

A solo funeral singer in Lewisham starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Lewisham?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Lewisham — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Lewisham?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Lewisham?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Lewisham. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
