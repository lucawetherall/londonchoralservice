Funeral singers and choirs in Bexley

The London Borough of Bexley sits on London’s south-eastern edge, where the city meets the green landscapes of Kent. From the historic village of Bexley to the communities of Erith, Bexleyheath, and Sidcup, we provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies at venues across the borough.

Funeral music in Bexley

St Mary the Virgin Bexley is one of the borough’s most beautiful and historically significant churches, with parts of the building dating back to the thirteenth century. Its tranquil setting beside the River Cray and its warm stone interior make it a deeply moving place for a funeral or memorial service, and our singers are honoured to perform there. The church’s acoustic is particularly well suited to small vocal ensembles, where the clarity of each voice can be fully appreciated. Erith Crematorium, serving families across Bexley and the surrounding area, is another venue where we regularly provide singers, and we are well versed in the particular requirements of crematorium services. For help choosing the right music, please see our funeral music guide or contact us to discuss your needs.

We also provide funeral singers for services at churches and chapels in Bexleyheath, where the town’s parish churches offer welcoming spaces for services of all sizes, and in Sidcup, where families often seek musicians who can bring a professional but personal touch to the occasion. Whether the service calls for a single hymn accompanied by organ or a full programme of choral music, we will help you find the right ensemble and repertoire. Our singers liaise directly with funeral directors and clergy to ensure seamless preparation. Visit our services page to learn more about our approach.

Wedding choirs in Bexley

Bexley’s village churches and historic parish settings provide a charming backdrop for weddings, and live choral music brings an atmosphere that recorded music simply cannot achieve. St Mary the Virgin Bexley is a particularly romantic choice, while the churches of Bexleyheath, Sidcup, and Crayford each have their own distinctive character. Our singers will work with you to plan a programme of hymns, anthems, and choral pieces that makes your ceremony truly memorable. Browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns for inspiration.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
