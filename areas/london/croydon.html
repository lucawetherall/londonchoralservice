Funeral singers and choirs in Croydon

Croydon is one of South London’s largest and most populous boroughs, with a rich history that stretches back to Saxon times. We provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies at churches, crematoria, and cemeteries throughout the borough, from the town centre to Purley, Sanderstead, and South Norwood.

Funeral music in Croydon

Croydon Minster, formally the Parish Church of St John the Baptist, is the borough’s most prominent place of worship and a magnificent setting for funeral and memorial services. Its spacious interior and fine organ create an acoustic that is both grand and intimate, well suited to choral music of every kind. We have provided singers for many services at the Minster, from large civic occasions to quiet family farewells. Croydon Crematorium, set in well-tended grounds off Mitcham Road, is another venue where our musicians are regularly called upon, and we understand the particular sensitivity and timing that crematorium services demand. For guidance on selecting the right music, please see our funeral music guide or get in touch with us directly.

Beyond these principal venues, we provide funeral singers for services at St John the Baptist Old Town — one of Croydon’s oldest surviving churches, with a peaceful churchyard and a warm acoustic — and at Mitcham Road Cemetery for graveside services and committal rites. We also cover churches and ceremony venues in Addington, Shirley, Thornton Heath, and across the wider borough. Our singers work in close coordination with funeral directors and clergy to ensure that every aspect of the music is arranged with care. Visit our services page to learn more about what we offer.

Wedding choirs in Croydon

From the grandeur of Croydon Minster to the quaint charm of smaller parish churches in Sanderstead, Purley, and Selsdon, the borough offers couples a fine choice of wedding venues. A professional choir brings an unmistakable quality to any wedding ceremony, filling the church with sound that recorded music cannot replicate. Our singers will help you choose hymns, anthems, and choral pieces that suit both the venue and the tone of your celebration. For ideas and inspiration, browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Croydon?

A solo funeral singer in Croydon starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150 and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Croydon?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Croydon — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Croydon?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Croydon?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Croydon. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
