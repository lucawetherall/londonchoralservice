Funeral singers and choirs in Barnet

The London Borough of Barnet stretches across the northern reaches of the capital, encompassing communities from Chipping Barnet and High Barnet in the north to Golders Green, Finchley, and Hendon in the south. Our conservatoire-trained singers bring dignified, expressive music to funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies at venues throughout this diverse borough.

Funeral music in Barnet

Barnet is home to some of north London’s most important funeral venues, and our singers perform regularly at many of them. Hendon Crematorium, set within its peaceful grounds off Holders Hill Road, is one of the borough’s busiest crematoriums, and we understand its layout, acoustics, and timing requirements well. Golders Green Crematorium — one of London’s oldest and most distinguished — has welcomed our singers for services ranging from intimate family farewells to larger memorial gatherings. We also sing at East Finchley Cemetery and at parish churches including St John the Baptist in Chipping Barnet, a medieval church with a resonant nave that suits choral music beautifully, and St Mary at Finchley, whose warm acoustic lends itself to both hymns and contemplative anthems.

Whether you are planning a traditional Church of England funeral, a non-religious ceremony, or a service that blends elements of both, we can help you choose music that feels right. Our funeral music guide offers suggestions for hymns, anthems, and reflective pieces, and we are always happy to learn new repertoire on request. To discuss your requirements, please visit our services page or get in touch directly.

Wedding choirs in Barnet

Barnet offers a wonderful range of settings for wedding ceremonies, from the historic grandeur of St John the Baptist in Chipping Barnet to the elegant simplicity of St Mary at Finchley. Many couples also choose licensed venues across the borough, including country house hotels and restored halls, where our singers can provide anything from a single soloist during the signing of the register to a full choir processional. We regularly help couples select hymns that their guests will know and enjoy alongside choral pieces that add a touch of something special. Our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a helpful starting point.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Barnet?

A solo funeral singer in Barnet starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Barnet?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Barnet — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Barnet?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Barnet?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Barnet. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
