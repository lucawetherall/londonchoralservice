Funeral singers and choirs in Ealing

Known as the “Queen of the Suburbs,” the London Borough of Ealing is a leafy and diverse corner of west London with a proud community spirit. The London Choral Service provides conservatoire-trained singers and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across Ealing, Greenford, Hanwell, Northolt, and Southall.

Funeral music in Ealing

We regularly sing at services throughout the borough, from the historic St Mary’s Ealing — one of west London’s finest parish churches — to South West Middlesex Crematorium and Greenford Park Cemetery. Our singers are equally at home in large, resonant naves and in the quieter setting of a crematorium chapel, adjusting their sound and repertoire to suit each space. Whether you would like traditional hymns, a choral setting of a psalm, or a well-loved song arranged for voices, our funeral music guide can help you explore your options, and we are always happy to discuss your ideas by phone or email. Please get in touch at any time.

Families across Ealing trust us to bring warmth and professionalism to the most important of days. We liaise directly with funeral directors and clergy so that the musical elements of the service run seamlessly, and we arrive in good time to rehearse and settle into the acoustic. From a single soloist singing Pie Jesu to a quartet performing a favourite hymn, we tailor every detail to your wishes. You can find a full overview of how we work on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Ealing

Ealing’s variety of wedding venues makes it easy to find a setting that reflects your personality as a couple. The elegant interior of St Mary’s Ealing provides a traditional backdrop for a choral wedding, while the Regency splendour of Pitzhanger Manor offers something more intimate and distinctive. Our singers can also perform at licensed venues across the borough, bringing beautiful live music to civil ceremonies and receptions alike. For inspiration on hymns, anthems, and processional music, take a look at our guide to choosing wedding hymns.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Ealing?

A solo funeral singer in Ealing starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Ealing?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Ealing — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Ealing?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Ealing?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Ealing. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
