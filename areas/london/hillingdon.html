Funeral singers and choirs in Hillingdon

The London Borough of Hillingdon is the westernmost borough in London, encompassing the market town of Uxbridge, the villages of Ruislip and Ickenham, and a patchwork of green belt countryside. The London Choral Service provides conservatoire-trained singers and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across the borough.

Funeral music in Hillingdon

We perform at venues throughout Hillingdon, including Breakspear Crematorium — one of north-west London’s busiest crematoria — Hillingdon Cemetery, and St John’s Hillingdon. Our singers also work at churches and chapels in Uxbridge, Ruislip, Hayes, and West Drayton. Whether the service is a traditional Church of England funeral, a Catholic requiem, or a non-religious celebration of life, we adapt our repertoire and our approach to suit the family’s wishes. Our funeral music guide is a useful starting point, and you are welcome to contact us at any stage of your planning.

Breakspear Crematorium’s chapel can feel sparse without live music, and families frequently tell us how much difference our singers make to the atmosphere. A well-chosen hymn or a beautifully sung solo lifts a service in a way that recorded music rarely can. We coordinate with funeral directors and celebrants to ensure seamless timing and communicate clearly about every detail beforehand. For a full description of how we work, please visit our services page.

Wedding choirs in Hillingdon

Hillingdon’s mix of historic churches and modern venues gives couples plenty of choice. St John’s Hillingdon is a popular choice for a traditional church wedding with full choral music, while Uxbridge offers a range of licensed venues for civil ceremonies. Whether you dream of a rousing rendition of Jerusalem or a gentle a cappella anthem, our singers can bring your vision to life. Take a look at our guide to choosing wedding hymns for practical ideas and inspiration.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
