Funeral singers and choirs in Wandsworth

Wandsworth encompasses some of South West London’s most desirable neighbourhoods, from the riverside elegance of Putney and the green spaces of Wandsworth Common to the vibrant streets of Battersea and Tooting. We provide conservatoire-trained singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies at venues across the borough.

Funeral music in Wandsworth

Putney Vale Crematorium, set within extensive grounds bordering Wimbledon Common, is one of London’s most well-known crematoria and a venue where our singers have performed on numerous occasions. Its chapels offer a dignified and tranquil setting, and the measured acoustic responds well to both solo voices and small choral ensembles. All Saints Church on Wandsworth High Street, with its striking tower and spacious interior, is another venue we know well — its warm acoustic makes it an ideal setting for traditional funeral services with hymns and choral music. For advice on selecting the right pieces, please see our funeral music guide or get in touch directly.

Across the borough, St Mary’s Battersea stands on a beautiful riverside site that has been a place of worship for over a thousand years. Its intimate chapel and fine views over the Thames provide a uniquely contemplative atmosphere for funeral and memorial services. Whether the service takes place in one of these historic churches, at Putney Vale, or in one of the many community venues around Battersea Park and beyond, we work closely with funeral directors, clergy, and families to ensure that every musical detail is handled with care. You can learn more about our approach on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Wandsworth

Wandsworth offers couples a wonderful variety of wedding settings. St Mary’s Battersea, where the poet and artist William Blake was married in 1782, holds a special place in the hearts of many couples, while All Saints Wandsworth and the churches of Putney provide handsome and welcoming spaces for ceremonies of all sizes. A live choir lifts a wedding ceremony in a way that no recording can, filling the building with warmth and creating moments of genuine emotion. Our singers are confident leading congregational hymns and equally at home performing intricate choral works. Browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns for inspiration.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
