Funeral singers and choirs in Waltham Forest

The London Borough of Waltham Forest stretches from Walthamstow and Leyton in the south to Chingford in the north, where the borough meets the ancient woodland of Epping Forest. Our conservatoire-trained singers provide sensitive, expertly prepared music for funerals, weddings, and memorial services at churches, cemeteries, and ceremony venues across the borough.

Funeral music in Waltham Forest

Waltham Forest’s funeral venues reflect the borough’s character — from busy urban centres to the quieter, greener reaches near the forest. Chingford Mount Cemetery is one of the borough’s principal cemeteries and a venue where our singers have performed on many occasions, providing everything from a solo voice at a graveside to a quartet in the cemetery chapel. Queens Road Cemetery in Walthamstow is another important local venue, and its chapel provides a respectful, contained space where live singing can make a remarkable difference to the atmosphere of a service. St Mary’s Walthamstow, the ancient parish church standing at the edge of Walthamstow Village, is a beautiful setting for funeral services — its medieval origins and peaceful churchyard create a sense of continuity and calm that families find deeply comforting.

We sing music from every tradition and period, from traditional hymns and Anglican choral works to contemporary songs and classical pieces. If a family has a particular piece in mind — perhaps a song that held special meaning for the person being remembered — we are always happy to learn it. Our funeral music guide is a good place to begin exploring options. You can find out more about our full range of offerings on our services page, or get in touch to talk things through.

Wedding choirs in Waltham Forest

Walthamstow has become one of east London’s most popular areas for weddings, and St Mary’s Walthamstow is a charming church for couples seeking a traditional ceremony with the uplift of live choral music. The borough’s licensed venues, including the William Morris Gallery and a number of stylish local spaces, offer further options for civil ceremonies where our singers can perform during the service or provide music for a reception. We enjoy helping couples find the right music for their day and are happy to suggest repertoire or accommodate special requests. Our guide to choosing wedding hymns offers a helpful introduction.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Waltham Forest?

A solo funeral singer in Waltham Forest starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Waltham Forest?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Waltham Forest — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Waltham Forest?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Waltham Forest?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Waltham Forest. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
