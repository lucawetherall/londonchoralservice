Funeral singers and choirs in Hackney

The London Borough of Hackney is one of east London’s most culturally rich boroughs, home to communities in Stoke Newington, Dalston, Clapton, and Shoreditch. Our conservatoire-trained singers provide thoughtful, beautifully delivered music for funerals, weddings, and memorial services at churches, cemeteries, and ceremony venues throughout Hackney.

Funeral music in Hackney

Hackney’s churches and cemeteries span centuries of London history, and each brings its own character to a funeral or memorial service. St John at Hackney, the magnificent Georgian church on Mare Street, has a soaring interior and a superb acoustic that can make even a small choir sound majestic. It is one of our favourite venues in east London to sing in. Abney Park Cemetery in Stoke Newington — a historic garden cemetery and nature reserve — offers a unique and atmospheric setting for farewell services, and our singers have performed there for families seeking something reflective and personal. St Mary of Eton, tucked beside the Hackney Marshes, provides a quieter, more intimate space, while the churches of Stoke Newington offer a range of settings suited to both traditional and contemporary services.

Whatever the style of the service, our singers can bring the right music to the occasion. We perform hymns, choral anthems, classical arias, and songs from other traditions, and we are always willing to learn unfamiliar pieces. Our funeral music guide is a useful resource if you are beginning to think about repertoire. You can learn more about what we offer on our services page, or simply contact us to start a conversation.

Wedding choirs in Hackney

Hackney has become one of London’s most sought-after boroughs for weddings, and its venues reflect the area’s creative, independent spirit. St John at Hackney is a magnificent church for a wedding ceremony, and its acoustic makes congregational singing feel effortless and uplifting. For couples choosing civil ceremonies, Hackney’s many licensed venues — from restored warehouses to elegant town halls — offer settings where a vocal soloist or small ensemble can create something truly memorable. We help couples plan their music from the first enquiry through to the day itself. Our guide to choosing wedding hymns offers practical advice for getting started.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
