Funeral singers and choirs in the City of London

The Square Mile is home to some of the finest ecclesiastical architecture in England. We provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, and memorial services at the City’s historic churches and livery halls.

Funeral music in the City of London

The City of London’s ancient churches provide dignified and beautiful settings for funeral and memorial services. St Paul’s Cathedral, Wren’s masterpiece on Ludgate Hill, is one of the most awe-inspiring spaces in which our singers perform. Its vast dome and magnificent acoustic call for musicians who can fill the space with confidence and reverence — qualities our ensembles bring to every service.

Beyond St Paul’s, we regularly provide funeral singers at Temple Church, the round church of the Knights Templar that sits between Fleet Street and the Thames. Its remarkable acoustic rewards careful, skilful singing. We also serve families holding services at St Bartholomew-the-Great in Smithfield, London’s oldest surviving parish church, and at St Mary-le-Bow on Cheapside — the church whose bells traditionally define a true Cockney. For families who prefer a non-religious setting, the City of London Crematorium in Manor Park offers a peaceful alternative, and our singers are experienced in providing music for services there.

Planning a funeral or memorial in the City can feel daunting, but we are here to help at every stage. Our funeral music guide offers practical advice, or you are welcome to contact us directly to talk through your needs.

Wedding choirs in the City of London

The City’s churches offer magnificent settings for weddings, and a professional choir can make the occasion truly unforgettable. Temple Church is a particularly sought-after wedding venue, its Norman round nave providing a breathtaking backdrop for the ceremony. St Bartholomew-the-Great and the Dutch Church at Austin Friars are equally stunning choices, each with its own distinctive character and acoustic.

Our wedding choirs range from small vocal ensembles to larger groups, and we tailor the repertoire to reflect each couple’s tastes — whether that means traditional Anglican hymns, Renaissance polyphony, or contemporary songs arranged for voices. For ideas and inspiration, explore our guide to choosing wedding hymns.

Ensembles for every occasion

Whether you need a solo singer for a quiet committal, a quartet for a church funeral, or a full choir for a grand City wedding, we can provide the right ensemble for your occasion and venue. All our musicians are professional singers with experience in the City’s churches and ceremonial spaces. Visit our pricing page for details on ensemble sizes and fees.
