Funeral singers and choirs in Newham

Newham lies at the heart of East London, stretching from the regenerated landscapes of Stratford and the Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park to the established communities of East Ham, West Ham, and Manor Park. We provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies at venues across the borough.

Funeral music in Newham

East London Crematorium on the borders of Newham and Redbridge is one of the busiest in the capital, and our singers have performed there on many occasions. The chapel’s measured acoustic suits a wide range of repertoire, from traditional hymns to contemporary songs, and we take care to tailor every performance to the wishes of the family. Nearby, the City of London Cemetery and Crematorium in Manor Park offers a grand and peaceful setting among landscaped gardens, with a chapel that responds beautifully to choral singing. St Mary Magdalene in East Ham, a fine medieval church with a warm and welcoming interior, is another venue where we regularly provide funeral music. For help choosing the right pieces, please see our funeral music guide or get in touch directly.

Whether a service takes place in a historic parish church, a modern crematorium chapel, or a community venue in Stratford or West Ham, we work closely with funeral directors, clergy, and families to ensure that every musical detail is handled with sensitivity and professionalism. Our singers are experienced in services of all faiths and none, and we are happy to learn specific pieces on request. You can learn more about our approach on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Newham

Newham’s transformation in recent years has brought striking new venues alongside the borough’s established churches. Whether you are planning a traditional ceremony at St Mary Magdalene East Ham or a contemporary celebration in one of Stratford’s modern event spaces, a live choir can bring a sense of occasion that recorded music simply cannot match. Our singers are skilled at leading congregational hymns with confidence while also delivering polished choral pieces during the signing of the register or as guests arrive. Browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns for inspiration.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Newham?

A solo funeral singer in Newham starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Newham?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Newham — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Newham?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Newham?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Newham. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
