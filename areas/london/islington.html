Funeral singers and choirs in Islington

The London Borough of Islington stretches from the bustle of Angel and Clerkenwell to the quieter reaches of Highbury and Holloway. We provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, and memorial services at churches and venues throughout the borough.

Funeral music in Islington

Islington’s churches and chapels offer a variety of settings for funeral and memorial services, from grand Victorian landmarks to peaceful neighbourhood parishes. Union Chapel on Compton Terrace is one of north London’s most striking buildings — a soaring Gothic chapel with an extraordinary octagonal interior and a rich, enveloping acoustic. Although best known today as a music venue, it remains a working Congregational church, and our singers are experienced in providing music for services held within its remarkable space.

St Mary’s Islington, the borough’s ancient parish church on Upper Street, has stood on its site since at least the twelfth century. The present Georgian building, with its graceful spire and light-filled interior, provides a dignified and welcoming setting for funeral services. In Clerkenwell, St James Church on Clerkenwell Close — one of the few surviving churches by James Carr — offers a more intimate atmosphere, well suited to smaller gatherings. We also regularly provide singers for services at Islington Cemetery on High Road in East Finchley, where our musicians bring warmth and professionalism to chapel and graveside services.

For guidance on selecting appropriate music, please see our funeral music guide, or contact us to discuss your needs.

Wedding choirs in Islington

Islington has become one of London’s most fashionable boroughs for weddings, and its churches provide settings that are both characterful and beautiful. Union Chapel’s dramatic interior makes it a truly memorable wedding venue, while St Mary’s Islington offers a more traditional church setting with excellent transport links. Our wedding choirs and vocal ensembles bring life and emotion to the ceremony, performing everything from classic hymns and Renaissance motets to contemporary pieces arranged specially for voices.

We tailor every programme to the couple’s wishes, and we are happy to suggest repertoire that works well in each venue’s acoustic. For ideas, explore our guide to choosing wedding hymns.

Ensembles for every occasion

Whether you require a solo singer for a graveside committal at Islington Cemetery, a duet or trio for a chapel service, or a full choir for a wedding at Union Chapel, we can provide the right ensemble. Our musicians are professional singers with wide experience in Islington’s churches and ceremonial venues. For details on ensemble sizes and fees, please visit our pricing page.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Islington?

A solo funeral singer in Islington starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Islington?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Islington — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Islington?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Islington?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Islington. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
