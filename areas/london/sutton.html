Funeral singers and choirs in Sutton

The London Borough of Sutton sits on London’s southern edge, where the suburbs give way to the chalk downlands of the North Downs. From the town centre of Sutton itself to the historic village of Carshalton and the quiet residential streets of Cheam, we provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across the borough.

Funeral music in Sutton

North East Surrey Crematorium, situated on the border of Sutton and neighbouring Morden, is the principal crematorium serving families in the borough. Its chapels are well designed for live music, and our singers have performed there on many occasions, delivering everything from traditional hymns and sacred anthems to popular songs and modern classical works. Sutton Cemetery, with its peaceful grounds and well-maintained chapel, provides another setting where live singing can bring real comfort and dignity to a funeral service. For guidance on choosing the right music, please see our funeral music guide or get in touch directly.

All Saints Church in Carshalton is one of the borough’s finest churches, standing beside the historic ponds in the heart of the village. Its generous acoustic and beautiful interior make it an ideal venue for funeral and memorial services with choral music. The churches of Cheam and Sutton town centre also offer warm and welcoming settings. We work closely with funeral directors, clergy, and families to ensure that every element of the music is prepared and performed with the utmost care. Our singers are experienced in services of all faiths and none, and we are always willing to learn unfamiliar pieces on request. You can learn more about our approach on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Sutton

Sutton’s churches offer couples some charming settings for a wedding ceremony. All Saints Carshalton, with its village setting and handsome interior, is a particularly popular choice, while the churches of Cheam and Sutton provide welcoming and well-appointed spaces for celebrations large and small. A live choir adds a dimension to a wedding that recorded music simply cannot achieve — the sound of trained voices rising together creates moments of real beauty and emotion. Our singers are equally confident leading congregational singing and performing polished choral pieces during quieter parts of the service. Browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns for inspiration.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
