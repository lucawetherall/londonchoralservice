Funeral singers and choirs in Merton

The London Borough of Merton is perhaps best known for Wimbledon and its famous common, but it also encompasses the neighbourhoods of Mitcham, Morden, and Colliers Wood, each with its own distinct character. We provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies at venues throughout the borough.

Funeral music in Merton

South London Crematorium in Mitcham is one of the principal crematoria serving families in Merton and the wider South London area. Its well-maintained chapels provide a dignified and peaceful setting for funeral services, and our singers have performed there on many occasions, adapting their repertoire to suit each family’s wishes — from traditional hymns and sacred choral music to popular songs and contemporary pieces. Morden Cemetery, with its spacious chapel and tranquil grounds, is another venue where we regularly provide live music for funerals and memorial services. For help choosing the right pieces, please see our funeral music guide or get in touch directly.

The borough’s churches also offer beautiful settings for funeral services. Wimbledon Parish Church — St Mary’s — stands on a hilltop site overlooking the village, and its fine acoustic and handsome interior make it an ideal venue for services with choral music. Churches in Colliers Wood and across the borough each have their own character and warmth. We work closely with funeral directors, clergy, and families to ensure that every detail of the music is handled with sensitivity and professionalism. You can learn more about our approach on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Merton

Merton’s churches provide some lovely settings for weddings. St Mary’s Wimbledon, perched on its hilltop with views across the village, is a particularly popular choice for couples seeking a traditional church ceremony in an area with real character. A live choir brings an unmistakable sense of occasion to any wedding, filling the church with voices in harmony and creating an atmosphere that guests will remember long after the day. Our singers are equally at home leading robust congregational hymns and performing refined choral works during the signing of the register. Browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns for inspiration.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
