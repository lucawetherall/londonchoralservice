Funeral singers and choirs in Kensington & Chelsea

The Royal Borough of Kensington and Chelsea is home to some of London’s most magnificent churches. We provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, and memorial services at venues across the borough, from the grandeur of Brompton Oratory to the charm of Chelsea Old Church.

Funeral music in Kensington & Chelsea

The churches of Kensington and Chelsea offer a range of beautiful settings for funeral and memorial services. Brompton Oratory — the London Oratory on Brompton Road — is one of the finest Catholic churches in England, and its magnificent Italianate interior and generous acoustic make it a remarkable venue for choral music. Our singers are well acquainted with its liturgical traditions and can provide appropriate music for requiem masses and funeral services in the Catholic rite.

For Church of England services, Holy Trinity Brompton and St Mary Abbots in Kensington High Street are among the borough’s most distinguished parish churches. Chelsea Old Church, beautifully rebuilt after wartime bomb damage, sits quietly on the Embankment and provides an intimate setting for smaller services. We also provide singers for funerals at St Luke’s Chelsea on Sydney Street, a striking example of early Gothic Revival architecture with a warm, clear acoustic.

Choosing music for a funeral can feel overwhelming, but our team is always happy to help. Our funeral music guide is a good place to start, or please contact us to discuss your requirements in confidence.

Wedding choirs in Kensington & Chelsea

Kensington and Chelsea’s churches make stunning wedding venues, and live choral music adds a layer of beauty and emotion that recorded music simply cannot match. Brompton Oratory is one of London’s most popular wedding churches, and our singers regularly perform there, providing everything from a small vocal ensemble to a full choir with orchestral accompaniment. Holy Trinity Brompton and St Luke’s Chelsea are equally popular choices for couples seeking a memorable ceremony.

We work with each couple to create a bespoke programme of music, drawing on centuries of choral tradition as well as contemporary repertoire. For help selecting hymns and anthems, see our guide to choosing wedding hymns.

Ensembles for every occasion

From a solo vocalist for a private memorial to a full choir for a grand Oratory wedding, we offer ensembles of every size to suit your venue, occasion, and budget. All our singers are experienced professionals drawn from London’s leading choral ensembles. For details on our ensemble options, please see our pricing page.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Kensington and Chelsea?

A solo funeral singer in Kensington and Chelsea starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Kensington and Chelsea?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Kensington and Chelsea — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Kensington and Chelsea?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Kensington and Chelsea?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Kensington and Chelsea. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
