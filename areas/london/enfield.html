Funeral singers and choirs in Enfield

The London Borough of Enfield sits at the very top of London, where the city gives way to the green spaces of Hertfordshire. From Enfield Town and Palmers Green to Southgate and Edmonton, our professional singers provide heartfelt music for funerals, weddings, and memorial services at churches, crematoriums, and ceremony venues across the borough.

Funeral music in Enfield

Enfield Crematorium, located on Great Cambridge Road, is the principal crematorium serving the borough and the surrounding areas of north London. Our singers know its chapel well and are experienced in working within its service timings to deliver music that is both moving and unhurried. We also perform regularly at Edmonton Cemetery, where graveside services benefit greatly from the presence of a clear, unaccompanied voice or a small vocal ensemble. Parish churches across Enfield offer beautiful settings for funeral services — St Andrew’s Enfield, with its striking tower and spacious interior, provides a fine acoustic for choral singing, while Christ Church Southgate, set amid its leafy churchyard, is an intimate and dignified venue for smaller gatherings.

We sing hymns, anthems, classical pieces, and contemporary songs, and we are happy to learn any special requests. If you are unsure where to begin, our funeral music guide offers practical suggestions for choosing music that reflects the life being celebrated. You can also view the full range of what we offer on our services page, or contact us to talk through your plans in detail.

Wedding choirs in Enfield

Enfield’s churches and licensed venues make wonderful settings for wedding ceremonies. St Andrew’s Enfield is a popular choice for couples seeking a traditional church wedding with the uplift of live choral music, and Christ Church Southgate offers a charming, more intimate alternative. Beyond churches, the borough has a number of licensed wedding venues where our singers can perform during the ceremony itself or provide background music for drinks receptions. We work closely with couples to choose hymns and choral pieces that suit the tone of their day — our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a popular starting point for ideas.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Enfield?

A solo funeral singer in Enfield starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Enfield?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Enfield — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Enfield?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Enfield?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Enfield. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
