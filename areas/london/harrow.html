Funeral singers and choirs in Harrow

The London Borough of Harrow sits on the north-western edge of the capital, crowned by the famous spire of St Mary’s on Harrow-on-the-Hill. The London Choral Service provides conservatoire-trained singers and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across Harrow, Pinner, Stanmore, and the surrounding neighbourhoods.

Funeral music in Harrow

We sing at venues throughout the borough, from the historic St Mary’s Harrow-on-the-Hill — a church with roots stretching back to the eleventh century — to Breakspear Crematorium, which serves families from across north-west London. Our singers also perform at parish churches in Pinner, Stanmore, Kenton, and Wealdstone, adapting their repertoire to reflect the wishes of each family. Whether you would like traditional hymns, a choral psalm, or a contemporary song arranged for voices, our funeral music guide can help you explore your options. Please get in touch whenever you are ready to discuss your plans.

Harrow’s diverse community means that our singers are experienced in serving families of many faiths and traditions. We bring the same respect, sensitivity, and musical polish to every service, whether it is a Church of England funeral, a secular celebration of life, or a memorial ceremony. We liaise with funeral directors and clergy to ensure that everything runs smoothly, and we always arrive with plenty of time to prepare. For more detail on how we work, visit our services page.

Wedding choirs in Harrow

St Mary’s Harrow-on-the-Hill, perched dramatically above the surrounding streets, is one of north-west London’s most atmospheric wedding venues. Its fine acoustic rewards choral singing, and our ensembles have performed there many times. Churches in Pinner and Stanmore also offer beautiful settings for a wedding with live music, and we are equally happy to sing at licensed venues for civil ceremonies. For help choosing hymns and anthems that reflect your taste, take a look at our guide to choosing wedding hymns.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
