Funeral singers and choirs in Haringey

Spanning from the leafy heights of Highgate and Muswell Hill to the vibrant communities of Tottenham and Wood Green, the London Borough of Haringey is one of north London’s most varied boroughs. Our professional singers provide sensitive, beautifully prepared music for funerals, weddings, and memorial services at venues across Haringey and its neighbouring areas.

Funeral music in Haringey

Haringey’s funeral venues range from grand Victorian churches to quiet cemetery chapels, and our singers are at home in all of them. St Augustine’s Highgate, perched near the top of Highgate Hill, is one of London’s finest Victorian churches, and its generous acoustic rewards choral music with a warmth and resonance that can deeply move a congregation. Tottenham Cemetery on Church Lane serves the eastern half of the borough and is a venue where we have sung for many families, often providing a soloist or duet at the graveside or in the cemetery chapel. New Southgate Cemetery, which borders the borough to the north, is another venue we know well. In Muswell Hill, the local churches offer beautiful spaces for funeral services where the presence of live voices can transform the atmosphere entirely.

We can provide music for services of any tradition or none — from traditional Anglican funerals with well-loved hymns to secular celebrations of life with contemporary songs and classical pieces. Our funeral music guide can help you begin choosing repertoire, and our services page explains what we offer in more detail. Please get in touch whenever you are ready to discuss your plans.

Wedding choirs in Haringey

From the dramatic setting of St Augustine’s Highgate to the quieter charms of parish churches in Muswell Hill and Crouch End, Haringey has much to offer couples planning a wedding with live music. Our singers can lead congregational hymns with confidence, perform motets and anthems during the signing of the register, and provide a joyful recessional as the newly married couple leaves the church. For civil ceremonies at licensed venues across the borough, we adapt our repertoire to suit the setting and the couple’s wishes. Browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns for inspiration.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Haringey?

A solo funeral singer in Haringey starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within Greater London and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Haringey?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Haringey — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the borough. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Haringey?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Haringey?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums across London including those in and around Haringey. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
