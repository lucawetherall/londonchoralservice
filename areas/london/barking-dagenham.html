Funeral singers and choirs in Barking and Dagenham

The London Borough of Barking and Dagenham stretches along the north bank of the Thames in East London, encompassing the historic town centre of Barking, the residential streets of Dagenham and Chadwell Heath, and a growing number of regeneration areas along the riverside. We provide professional singers and choirs for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies at venues throughout the borough.

Funeral music in Barking and Dagenham

St Margaret’s Church in Barking is one of the most historically significant churches in East London, standing on grounds that were once part of Barking Abbey, one of the great medieval monasteries of England. Its stone walls and high ceilings create a resonant acoustic that is ideally suited to choral singing, and our musicians have been privileged to perform at funeral and memorial services there on many occasions. Eastbrookend Cemetery and Rippleside Cemetery both serve families across the borough with well-maintained chapels where live music can make a profound difference to the atmosphere of a service. For guidance on choosing the right music, please see our funeral music guide or get in touch directly.

We also provide funeral singers for services in Chadwell Heath and at churches and community venues throughout Dagenham. Whether a family chooses traditional hymns, a contemporary song, or a piece of classical choral music, we take the time to prepare every performance with care and sensitivity. Our singers are experienced in services of all faiths and none, and we work closely with funeral directors and clergy to ensure that the music runs seamlessly. You can learn more about our approach on our services page.

Wedding choirs in Barking and Dagenham

For couples planning a wedding in Barking and Dagenham, the historic setting of St Margaret’s Church offers a truly memorable backdrop for a ceremony. The borough’s other churches and licensed venues also provide warm and welcoming spaces for celebrations of all sizes. A live choir brings an atmosphere that recorded music simply cannot replicate — the sound of voices in harmony filling a church is something guests remember for years. Our singers are skilled at supporting congregational hymns while also delivering polished choral pieces during the signing of the register. Browse our guide to choosing wedding hymns for inspiration.

Ensembles for every occasion

Not every occasion calls for the same size of ensemble, and part of our job is helping you find the right balance for your venue, your budget, and the feeling you want to create. We offer everything from a single soloist — whose voice can hold a room in complete stillness — through to duets, quartets, octets, and a full twelve-voice chorus with instrumental accompaniment. Organists, harpists, and string players are also available. Visit our pricing page for a clear guide to ensemble sizes and costs.
