Funeral singers and choirs in Bath

Bath is one of the most beautiful cities in the world — a place where golden Georgian stone, ancient hot springs, and centuries of worship come together in a setting of extraordinary grace. As a UNESCO World Heritage city, it carries a weight of history and architecture that few places in England can match. The London Choral Service provides professional singers, choirs, and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across Bath, the surrounding Somerset countryside, and nearby Bristol, bringing musicianship and warmth to occasions that deserve nothing less.

Funeral music in Bath

When someone you love has died, the last thing you should have to worry about is finding the right musicians. We understand how overwhelming it can be, and we are here to take care of the music so you can focus on being with your family. Our singers perform at funerals and memorial services throughout Bath and Somerset — at Bath Abbey, whose soaring fan-vaulted ceiling and magnificent acoustic make it one of the finest sacred spaces in England, at St Swithin’s Walcot, at St Mary’s Bathwick, at Haycombe Crematorium, and at parish churches across the county. We also serve families in nearby Bristol, where many choose to hold services in the city’s own historic churches and chapels. Whether you would like a solo soprano singing Ave Maria as the coffin is carried in, or a quartet lifting the congregation with a much-loved hymn, we shape every detail around what feels right for the person you are remembering and for the people who will be there.

Bath’s Georgian architecture and intimate church interiors give the music a particular resonance here. The stone walls and high ceilings of the city’s churches were built to carry sound, and a well-trained voice in these spaces is something truly moving. Every musician on our roster is conservatoire-trained, and many have sung in cathedral and collegiate choirs across the country. If you are not sure where to begin with choosing music, our funeral music guide offers thoughtful suggestions for hymns, solos, and choral works that families often find a real source of comfort. You are also very welcome to telephone us at any time — we are always happy to talk things through quietly and at your own pace, and there is never any obligation.

Wedding choirs and singers

It is hard to imagine a more romantic setting for a wedding than Bath. The honeyed crescents and colonnades, the candlelit interiors of its churches, the rolling green hills that frame the city on every side — everything about this place lends itself to celebration. Live singing transforms a wedding ceremony in a way that no recording ever could. A choir filling Bath Abbey with harmony, a soprano performing as the register is signed in a quiet parish church, a congregation raising their voices together for a hymn everyone knows — these are the moments guests talk about for years afterwards. Our choirs and soloists perform at weddings of every size and denomination across Bath and Somerset, and we are always happy to learn new repertoire if there is a piece that holds particular meaning for you. Our guide to choosing wedding hymns is a lovely place to start if you are still exploring your options.

Ensembles & pricing

We offer everything from a single soloist to a full choir of twelve voices, and can pair singers with an organist, harpist, or string quartet. Each musician is personally selected from our handpicked roster of professional singers and instrumentalists. Because every occasion is different, we encourage you to tell us a little about what you have in mind so we can recommend the right ensemble and provide an accurate quote. Full details of our standard packages are available on our pricing page.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a funeral singer cost in Bath?

A solo funeral singer in Bath starts from £215. A small choir of four voices starts from £1,150, a quintet of five from £1,400, a sextet of six from £1,600, and a full choir of eight from £2,000. All prices include travel within the local area and liaison with your funeral director.

Can you provide a choir for a wedding in Bath?

Yes, we provide wedding choirs of all sizes in Bath — from a solo vocalist to a chorus of twelve. Our singers perform at churches, hotels, and licensed venues across the area. We handle all the musical arrangements so you can focus on your day.

What types of music can you perform at a funeral in Bath?

We perform everything from traditional hymns and classical sacred music to contemporary songs and popular favourites. Our musicians can also create bespoke arrangements of any piece you have in mind. We are happy to advise on music choices if you are unsure.

Do you cover crematorium services in Bath?

Yes, we regularly perform at crematoriums in and around Bath. Our singers are experienced in adapting to the specific timing and acoustic requirements of crematorium services, and we liaise directly with staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.
