Privacy policy

How we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data.

Who we are

The London Choral Service is the operating name of Alma Consort Ltd. We provide singers, choirs, and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies. This policy explains how we handle your personal data when you use our website or contact us.

What we collect

When you contact us by enquiry form, email, or telephone, we may collect your name, email address, telephone number, and details about your occasion. We collect this information solely to respond to your enquiry and deliver our services.

How we use your information

We use your personal data to respond to your enquiry, arrange the services you have requested, and communicate with you about your booking. We never use your data for marketing unless you have given us explicit consent to do so.

How we store your information

Your data is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected. We never sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except where strictly necessary to deliver our services — for example, sharing venue details with our musicians for a confirmed booking.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of the personal data we hold about you. You may withdraw your consent for us to contact you at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@londonchoral.co.uk.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies of any kind. We use Google Fonts to serve typefaces, which involves requests to Google's servers. No analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services are used.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. This policy was last updated in February 2026.
