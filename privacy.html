Privacy policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Who we are

The London Choral Service provides professional singers and musicians for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies. This policy explains how we handle your personal data when you contact us or use our website.

What we collect

When you contact us via our enquiry form, email, or telephone, we may collect your name, email address, telephone number, and details about your occasion. We collect this information solely to respond to your enquiry and provide our services.

How we use your information

We use your personal information to respond to your enquiry, arrange the services you have requested, and communicate with you about your booking. We do not use your information for marketing purposes unless you have given explicit consent.

How we store your information

Your personal data is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except where necessary to provide our services (for example, sharing venue details with our musicians for a confirmed booking).

Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. You may also withdraw consent for us to contact you at any time. To exercise any of these rights, please email hello@londonchoral.co.uk.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies. We use Google Fonts to serve typefaces, which may involve requests to Google's servers. No analytics or advertising services are used.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. This policy was last updated in February 2026.
