Privacy policy

How we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data.

Who we are

The London Choral Service (operating name of Alma Consort Ltd) provides singers, choirs, and instrumentalists for funerals, weddings, memorials, and ceremonies across the UK. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains clearly how we handle your personal data when you use our website or contact us.

What we collect

When you contact us via our enquiry form, email, or telephone, we collect only what's necessary: your name, email address, phone number (if you provide it), and information about your occasion. We collect this data solely to respond to your enquiry and deliver the services you've asked for. Nothing more.

How we use your information

We use your data to respond to your enquiry, arrange your booking, and communicate about your service. We will never use your data for marketing, newsletters, or any other purpose without asking for your explicit consent first. No surprises.

How we store your information

Your data is stored securely and kept only as long as needed. We never sell, trade, or share your personal data with anyone — ever — except where absolutely necessary to deliver your booking (for example, sharing your venue details with the musicians performing at your event). That's it.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you can ask us to show you the personal data we hold, correct it, or delete it entirely. You can withdraw your consent to contact you at any time. Just email office@londonchoralservice.com and we'll handle it immediately.

Cookies and tracking

We don't use cookies or any tracking technologies. We don't use analytics, advertising pixels, or any third-party tracking services. The only external resource we load is Google Fonts for our typefaces. Your browsing on our site is completely private.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. This policy was last updated in February 2026.
