Services

From the most solemn farewells to the most joyful celebrations, we provide live music that rises to the occasion.

Funerals

The right music brings comfort that words alone cannot. Our singers lead congregational hymns with warmth and authority, giving everyone present — even those who haven't sung in years — the confidence to join in. Between hymns, solo pieces and choral works create moments of profound beauty and stillness.

We coordinate directly with your funeral director and officiant to ensure every note fits perfectly into the flow of the service. If you're unsure what to choose, we'll guide you through the options. We've helped hundreds of families through this, and we understand how much it matters to get it right.

Weddings

From the processional to the signing of the register, live music turns a ceremony into something genuinely unforgettable. We provide singers and instrumentalists for the service, and can continue through the reception, creating atmosphere for celebration and conversation.

Our repertoire spans classical, sacred, and contemporary music. If there's a piece you've always imagined hearing on your wedding day — whether it's a Bach chorale, a Boyz II Men song, or something unexpected — we can arrange it for your chosen ensemble. That level of personalisation is what sets the day apart.

Memorials & celebrations of life

A memorial or celebration of life should reflect the person being remembered — their personality, their passions, the feeling their family wants to create. Some choose reflective pieces. Others want joyful, uplifting music. Some want both. We listen carefully and shape the music around your vision.

We perform in every imaginable setting: ancient churches, modern community halls, private homes, gardens, marquees, and outdoor spaces. If you can hold a gathering, we can provide the music.

Corporate events & ceremonies

Live music transforms corporate occasions. Whether it's an award ceremony, gala dinner, commemorative service, or carol concert, the right music elevates the atmosphere and creates moments people remember. We work with your event coordinator to ensure perfect timing and tone.

Tell us about your event and we'll discuss how music can enhance it.

Our ensembles

Soloist
A single, exceptional voice for intimate settings and moments that call for simplicity and purity. One performance piece, plus leading congregation through up to three hymns.
Small Choir (4 singers)
Four-part harmony that fills a room with warmth and depth without overwhelming the space. Ideal for mid-sized venues and ceremonies of 50-200 people. One performance piece, plus leading congregation through up to three hymns.
Full Choir (8 singers)
A richer, more resonant sound for larger venues and occasions that need greater presence. Powerful without dominating the space. One performance piece, plus leading congregation through up to three hymns.
Chorus (12 singers)
The full choral experience: a powerful, moving ensemble that can fill a cathedral and create moments people never forget. Two performance pieces, plus leading congregation through up to four hymns.

Instrumentalists

Pianists, organists, harpists, and string quartets — either accompanying our singers or performing on their own. We tailor the instrumentation to your venue, your repertoire, and the character of the occasion.
