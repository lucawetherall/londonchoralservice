Services

From the most solemn farewells to the most joyful celebrations, we provide live music that rises to the occasion.

Funerals

The right music brings comfort that words alone cannot. Our singers lead congregational hymns with warmth and authority, giving everyone present the confidence to sing. Between the hymns, solo pieces and choral works bring moments of beauty and stillness to the service.

We coordinate directly with your funeral director and officiant to ensure the music fits seamlessly into the order of service. If you are unsure what to choose, we will guide you — we have helped hundreds of families through this process and understand how much it matters.

Weddings

From the processional to the signing of the register, live music turns a wedding ceremony into something truly memorable. We provide singers and instrumentalists for the service itself, and can continue through drinks receptions and the wedding breakfast.

Our repertoire covers the classical, the sacred, and the contemporary. If there is a piece you have always imagined hearing on your wedding day, we can arrange it for your chosen ensemble.

Memorials & celebrations of life

A memorial or celebration of life calls for music that genuinely reflects the person being remembered — their character, their tastes, the feeling their family wants to create. The tone might be reflective, joyful, or both. We take our lead from you.

We perform in every setting: churches, community halls, private homes, gardens, and more.

Corporate events & ceremonies

Choral and instrumental music for award ceremonies, gala dinners, commemorative services, carol concerts, and any occasion where live performance elevates the atmosphere.

Get in touch to discuss your requirements.

Our ensembles

Soloist
A single, exceptional voice for intimate settings. One performance piece, with congregation leading for up to three hymns.
Small Choir (4 singers)
Four-part harmony that fills a room with warmth and depth. One performance piece, with congregation leading for up to three hymns.
Full Choir (8 singers)
A richer, more resonant sound that suits larger venues and grander occasions. One performance piece, with congregation leading for up to three hymns.
Chorus (12 singers)
The full choral experience: powerful, moving, and unforgettable. Two performance pieces, with congregation leading for up to four hymns.

Instrumentalists

Pianists, organists, harpists, and string quartets — either accompanying our singers or performing on their own. We tailor the instrumentation to your venue, your repertoire, and the character of the occasion.
