Services

We provide music for all of life's most important occasions, from the solemn to the celebratory.

Funerals

Music can bring extraordinary comfort at a funeral. Our singers lead congregational hymns with confidence and warmth, ensuring that everyone present feels supported. We also perform solo pieces and choral works that bring beauty and solace to the service.

We work closely with funeral directors and officiants to ensure the music fits seamlessly into the order of service. If you're unsure what to choose, we're here to help — we've guided hundreds of families through this process.

Weddings

From the processional to the signing of the register, our musicians add elegance and emotion to your wedding day. We provide music for the ceremony itself, and can also perform during drinks receptions and wedding breakfasts.

Our repertoire spans the classical, the sacred, and the contemporary. If you have a particular piece in mind, we can arrange it for your chosen ensemble.

Memorials & celebrations of life

Memorial services and celebrations of life call for music that reflects the person being remembered. The tone can be reflective, uplifting, or a combination of both — we take our lead from you.

We perform in all settings: churches, community halls, private homes, outdoor spaces, and more.

Corporate events & ceremonies

We provide choral and instrumental music for award ceremonies, gala dinners, commemorative events, carol services, and other occasions where live music elevates the atmosphere.

Please get in touch to discuss your requirements.

Our ensembles

Soloist
A single professional voice for intimate occasions. One performance piece, with congregation leading for up to three hymns.
Small Choir (4 singers)
Rich four-part harmony that fills a room with warmth. One performance piece, with congregation leading for up to three hymns.
Full Choir (8 singers)
A fuller, more resonant sound suited to larger venues. One performance piece, with congregation leading for up to three hymns.
Chorus (12 singers)
The full choral experience: powerful, moving, and unforgettable. Two performance pieces, with congregation leading for up to four hymns.

Instrumentalists

We also provide pianists, organists, harpists, and string quartets, either accompanying our singers or as standalone performers. Instrumentation can be tailored to suit your venue and the character of the occasion.
