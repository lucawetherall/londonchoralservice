Most requested

Ave Maria — Soloist

Schubert's beloved setting — by far the most requested piece across all our services. Luminous, moving, and profoundly comforting. Traditionally performed by a solo soprano.

How Great Thou Art — Full Choir

A hymn of tremendous power. The choral harmonies build gradually, culminating in one of the most stirring, unforgettable climaxes in all congregational music. Genuinely transcendent.

Ave Verum Corpus — Full Choir

Mozart's sublime motet — just four minutes of extraordinary beauty, serenity, and grace. One of the perfect pieces of choral music ever written.

The most beloved hymn at funerals across the United Kingdom, sung here to the well-known Crimond melody. Gentle, familiar, and deeply comforting. A hymn that has comforted countless congregations.

A hymn of profound comfort. The final verse — “in life, in death, O Lord, abide with me” — is one of the most powerful moments in any funeral service. Sung with conviction, it offers genuine consolation.
